Projects

Stock Portfolio Tracker

This stock portfolio tracker web application is a way for users to keep track of their stock portfolio, offering a personalized dashboard for users to have a comprehensive overview of their portfolio history. The app is built using python, flask, and HTML/CSS. The statistical visualizations are made using Bokeh and the stock data is from the yfinance package. To use the app, just enter a stock ticker, a date, and how many shares of the stock was purchased.
